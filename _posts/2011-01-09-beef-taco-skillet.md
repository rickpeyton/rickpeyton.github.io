The Ingredients

- 1lb ground beef
- 10.75oz can tomato soup
- 1/2c salsa
- 1/2c water
- 6 6″ flour tortillas cut into 1″ pieces
- 1/2c shredded cheddar cheese

The Steps

- Brown beef
- Strip in soup, salsa, water, tortillas and heat to a boil
- Simmer for 5 minutes
- Top with cheese
